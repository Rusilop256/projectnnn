My Favorite Actors

Ryan Gosling

**Ryan Gosling** is a Canadian actor known for his charm and versatile roles. He rose to fame with *The Notebook* (2004) and starred in hits like *Drive* (2011), *La La Land* (2016), and *Blade Runner 2049* (2017). Beyond acting, he’s also a musician and leads a private life with his partner Eva Mendes and their two daughters.

Emma Stone

Emma Stone is an award-winning American actress known for her roles in *Easy A* (2010), *La La Land* (2016), and *The Help* (2011). With her wit and talent, she’s become one of Hollywood’s most beloved stars.

Robert Downey Jr.

Famous for his portrayal of Tony Stark in *Iron Man*, Robert Downey Jr. has redefined the superhero genre with his charismatic performances.

Dwayne Johnson

**Dwayne Johnson**, also known as "The Rock," is an American actor, producer, and former professional wrestler. Born on **May 2, 1972**, he gained fame in WWE before transitioning to Hollywood. Known for his charisma and action-packed roles, he has starred in hits like *Fast & Furious*, *Jumanji*, and *Black Adam*. Off-screen, he’s admired for his motivational outlook and philanthropy.

My Favorite Movies

The Incredible History of the Island of Roses

The Island of Roses is based on the true story of Giorgio Rosa, an Italian engineer who built a man-made island in international waters in the 1960s, declaring it an independent micro-nation. Despite issuing its own currency and gaining international attention, the Italian government eventually dismantled the island. The story is a tale of idealism and rebellion against bureaucracy.

Titanic

A timeless love story directed by James Cameron, *Titanic* remains one of the most iconic films in cinema history.

The Avengers

Marvel's *The Avengers* brings together iconic superheroes in an epic battle to save the world.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Martin Scorsese's *The Wolf of Wall Street* is a gripping tale of ambition, greed, and excess.

Green Book

*Green Book* is a 2018 film based on the true story of an unlikely friendship between African-American pianist **Don Shirley** and his Italian-American driver **Tony Lip**. Set in the 1960s, during the American Civil Rights Movement, the movie follows their journey through the segregated South. The film highlights themes of racism, tolerance, and friendship, with both characters learning from each other's differences. **Green Book** won several awards, including the Academy Award for Best Picture.
